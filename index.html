Climate Action Now

Share your voice with the world!

Your voice is needed now! It is our responsibility to show our political representatives, and the world, that we care about climate change and that they should too. The Climate Action Now campaign is a collaboration between the Sierra Club and Our Children’s Trust (Youth Climate Action Now—YouCAN). The campaign involves an intergenerational effort in policy making and climate activism--leveraging everyone’s talents, enthusiasm, and love for the world to shape policy decisions on a local, county, and state level. The YouCAN campaign involves youth, whose voice is underrepresented and under requested in politics. Yet, voices of youth continue to be the most powerful and represent the most ambitious generation yet.

Campaign Updates

What have we been up to?

2 February 2016

Tell the world!

The City of Corvallis has formed a group that is working on local climate issues--the Climate Action Task Force (CATF). The YouCAN Corvallis leadership team testified at the CATF meeting about the necessity of legally-binding, science-based emissions reduction targets. Afterward we received positive feedback from many people in the room, and we plan on returning often to these meetings to provide a youth-focused perspective on the CATF's work.
