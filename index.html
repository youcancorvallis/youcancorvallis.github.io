Why You?

Share your voice with the world!

As youth, our voice is underrepresented and under asked for in politcs and every other area of society. Yet, voices of youth continue to to be the most powerful and represent the most ambitous generation yet. As youth, it is our responsibility to show the world that we care about the climate and that they should too. YouCAN Corvallis is a campaign that involved youth in policy making sand climate activism, leveraging our talents, enthusiasm, and love for the world to shape policy decisions on a local, county, and state level.

Campaign Updates

What have we been up to?

2 February 2016

Tell the world!

The City has a group that is worling on climate issues in Corvallis--the Climate Action Task Force (CATF). The YouCAN Corvallis leadership team testified at the CATF meeting about the necessity of legally-binding, science-based emissions reduction targets. Afterward, we got positive feedback from many people in the room, and we plan on coming back often to provide a youth-focused perspective on the task force's work.
