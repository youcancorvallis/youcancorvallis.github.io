How can I help fight climate change in Corvallis?

You CAN help fight climate change: advocate for a Corvallis Climate Recovery Ordinance and binding GHG emission reduction targets. Local government must help us transition off fossil fuels and adapt to a changing climate. We simply need to ask the city to ensure protection of the atmosphere for future generations.

What is YouCAN Corvallis?

Youth Climate Action Now is a state-wide movement to empower youth and young adults to have their voices heard about their future and climate change. We are starting this movement in Corvallis because we believe that it is our time to set an example for other cities and states.

Our Children's Trust (OCT) has already done this work in Eugene, Oregon. There, after more than a year of youth empowerment and local engagement, the Climate Recovery Ordinance was passed, making Eugene the first in the nation to pass this kind of legally-binding emission target.
